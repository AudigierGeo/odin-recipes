The Greatest short nonsense Pork Pie Recipe Ever!!!

Not really much to say here, I don't actually know how to make a pork pie, but this one looks great and I would definitely eat it.

I would recoomend eating this either with just a blob of pickle, or maybe a little salad

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Fat
  • PAstry
  • Jelly
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Make the pastry and mould it into a shell
  2. Make the pork and fat into sausage meat
  3. Sal and pepper to taste
  4. Fill pastry mould
  5. Crimp down lid
  6. Bake
  7. Warm jelly and fill pie
